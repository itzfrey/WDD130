Overview

Purpose

The purpose of my rafting business is to increase visibility, attract customers, and streamline operations through online booking and communication, while showcasing your expertise and fostering customer engagement through valuable content and reviews.

Audience

Who are our target customers? Our target customers are adventure enthusiasts and outdoor lovers who seek thrilling experiences and memorable adventures. They are typically individuals or groups in the age range of 18-40, with moderate to high income levels, and a strong interest in outdoor activities and adrenaline-filled experiences. What do they want? They want to engage in exhilarating water rafting trips that provide an escape from their daily routines and allow them to connect with nature. They expect to find detailed information about the rafting trips, including the available routes, difficulty levels, safety measures, equipment provided, and pricing. What do they expect to find? By accessing the website, they hope to have their questions answered regarding the suitability of the rafting trips for their skill levels, the scenic beauty of the rivers, the duration and intensity of the trips, and any additional services or amenities offered.
